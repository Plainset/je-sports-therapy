About James

Meet James Egginton.

Sports Therapist and Soft Tissue Therapist, Leamington Spa.

James treats everyone from competitive powerlifters moving 170kg-plus off the floor to office workers nursing a stiff neck from a desk job — every session is built around the client in front of him, not a one-size-fits-all routine. Whether you're chasing a personal best or just want to move without pain again, treatment is tailored to what your body actually needs.

Training & qualifications

More than a decade of building the right skill set.

  1. 2012

    BSc Sports Science

    The academic foundation — how the body moves, loads, adapts and breaks down under training.

  2. 2014

    Qualified Soft Tissue Therapist

    Trained at the London School of Sports and Remedial Massage, building the hands-on manual therapy skills at the core of every treatment.

  3. 2024

    Qualified Sports Therapist

    Qualified with Active Health Group, sharpening assessment and rehab skills for sport-specific injury recovery.

  4. Specialisms

    RockDoc & Instrument-Assisted Soft Tissue

    Qualified RockDoc for kinesiology taping (Rocktape), plus a specialist in Instrument-Assisted Sports Massage — extra tools for stubborn, hard-to-shift issues.

On the touchline

3 seasons of pitch-side first aid with Broadstreet RFC.

James has spent three seasons pitch-side with Broadstreet RFC, a National 3 Midlands Premier rugby team — real-time first aid experience with contact-sport injuries at competitive level.
