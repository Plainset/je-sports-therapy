Parking

There's a handful of limited spaces directly outside the clinic. Otherwise, on-street paid parking is available on Portland Street (up to 2 hours), and the Covent Garden Multi-Storey Car Park is close by. Prefer free parking? Upper Grove Street and Beauchamp Hill both offer 2 hours free, a 5–10 minute walk from the clinic.

  • Outside clinic — limited spaces
  • Portland Street — paid, up to 2hrs
  • Covent Garden Multi-Storey
  • Upper Grove Street — free 2hrs
  • Beauchamp Hill — free 2hrs
